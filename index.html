Welcome to Amnesia

Project Amnesia is a community-driven, open-source Minecraft launcher built specifically to streamline the often chaotic world of modding. Designed with a strong focus on performance and user autonomy, it offers seamless integration with major platforms like CurseForge and Modrinth, allowing players to download, update, and manage complex modpacks with just a few clicks. Unlike heavier, ad-laden alternatives, Amnesia prides itself on a minimalist, highly customizable interface and a lightweight footprint that ensures your system's resources are dedicated to running the game smoothly. Because its code is entirely transparent, the launcher benefits from continuous community contributions, ensuring a secure, privacy-respecting, and robust foundation for both hardcore modders and casual players who want to take full control over their Minecraft experience.
