PROJECT RULES & GUIDELINES

GENERAL PROJECT POLICY

The following are strictly prohibited on the project in any form:
  • Nazism, fascism, extremism, and any form of discriminatory ideology.
  • Erotic, immoral, political, or military context.
  • Mentions of "death groups," calls for self-harm, or threats of real-life violence.
  • Promotion of psychotropic, narcotic, or nicotine-containing substances.
  • Profanity (including masked/censored swearing) and insults.
  • Advertising third-party resources and using a player's real-life data without their consent.

1. ACCOUNT & SECURITY

  • 1.1 Agreement: By registering, you agree to the Data Processing Policy and the License Agreement.
  • 1.2 Sharing: Transferring or selling your account to third parties is forbidden.
  • 1.3 Nicknames: Nicknames that violate project policy or impersonate staff (Admin, Moderator, etc.) are prohibited.
  • 1.4 Customization: Skins, capes, and prefixes must remain appropriate and follow general policy.
  • 1.5 Alts: Creating "twink" (alternative) accounts to gain gameplay advantages is prohibited.

2. IN-GAME ASSETS & BANS

  • 2.1 RMT: Trading in-game resources for real-world currency or outside values is forbidden.
  • 2.2 Refunds: In-game assets are not refundable upon account block. The service is provided "as is"—resources lost due to bugs, server errors, or player mistakes will not be returned.
  • 2.3 Authority: Staff reserves the right to seize resources or remove regions/warps if necessary for server health.
  • 2.4 Sanctions: Repeated violations may lead to a permanent ban of all associated accounts.

3. CHAT & COMMUNICATION

  • 3.1 Chat Cleanliness: No spamming, flooding, or excessive use of Caps Lock (messages where over 50% are capital letters).
  • 3.2 Topics: Discussing cheats, dupe glitches, or critical server exploits in public chat is prohibited.
  • 3.3 Commerce: Commercial messages (buying/selling) are allowed once every 5 minutes. Warp advertisements are allowed once every 10 minutes.
  • 3.4 Staff Interaction: Publicly criticizing or disputing staff actions is prohibited. Use the Complaint Section on the Forum for appeals.

4. REGIONS & BUILDINGS

  • 4.1 Responsibility: You are responsible for who you add to your private region.
  • 4.2 Distance: Do not create regions closer than 3 chunks to another player's region without their consent.
  • 4.3 Performance: Buildings that create excessive server load (lag machines) are strictly forbidden.
  • 4.4 Griefing: Intentionally ruining the map's appearance (cobblestone towers, lava casts, etc.) is prohibited.

5. ECONOMY & EVENTS

  • 5.1 Fair Combat: Killing a player is only allowed 2 minutes after they have teleported.
  • 5.2 Commands: Using /vanish, /tp, or similar staff-level commands to gain an advantage in combat is forbidden.
  • 5.3 Traps: Creating any form of player traps (e.g., portal traps, death warps) is prohibited.
  • 5.4 Cheating: Using third-party software, modified clients, or storing "duped" items will result in a ban.
  • 5.5 Entity Limits: A maximum of 8 mobs per chunk is allowed to ensure stability.

6. FAIR PLAY

  • 6.1 Pricing: Selling items below the established minimum price is forbidden. See Minimum Price List.
  • 6.2 Giveaways: Only basic resources (food, dirt, stone, glass) may be given away for free in large quantities.
  • 6.3 Player Events: Any player-made casinos or high-reward events must be pre-approved by Senior Management.
